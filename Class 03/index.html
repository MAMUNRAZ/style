Modern living room

In a modern living you can afford to have fun with functional furniture pieces. Turn functional storage into a display opportunity for all the things you want to show off. Dress with propped artwork, decorative vessels, plants and a reading lamp. Add interest to the walls behind with a panel of painted colour and giant hooks for hanging planters and baskets.Contemporary living rooms need modern storage solutions, that are as attractive as they are useful. A stack of floating shelves creates a wall of storage and display in a living space. Just add chairs for the perfect place to perch with a laptop and make the space work as a home office.
